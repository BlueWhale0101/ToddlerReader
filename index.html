Letter Adventure

A clickable prototype for toddler-friendly early reading play

Ages: toddler / preschool
Focus: sounds + letters
Prototype: clickable

Play with sounds.
Notice letters.
Build a name.

This prototype includes three gentle game modes: Find the Sound, Letter Hunt, and Name Builder. Everything is designed to feel simple, cheerful, and adult-guided.

🦊✨
🔊

Find the Sound

Hear a beginning sound, then tap the picture that matches. Example: “Find /d/” and choose the dog.

🌳

Letter Hunt

Explore a playful scene and tap something that starts with the target sound or letter.

🔤

Name Builder

Build your child’s name from a friendly letter bank, starting with the most meaningful word of all.

Child name

Prototype notes

This version is intentionally simple. It shows interaction design, feedback, and flow. A fuller version could add sound playback, progression, sticker collections, adaptive difficulty, and parent settings.
